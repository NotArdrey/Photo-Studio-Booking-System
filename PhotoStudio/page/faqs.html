About Captured By K

Captured By K is a self-photography studio located in Albay, Philippines, offering a creative space where clients can take professional-quality photos themselves. The studio provides high-end equipment, customizable backdrops, and lighting to create stunning portraits.

Studio Services

We offer a variety of services, including personal photo sessions, family portraits, and corporate headshots. Captured By K provides all the necessary tools and guidance to help clients capture their best moments with ease.

Why Choose Us?

Our studio is designed for those who want a comfortable and private setting to take photos. With our user-friendly setup and helpful staff, clients of all photography skill levels can achieve professional results.

How It Works

Clients can book a session online, choose their preferred time slot, and come to the studio for a self-guided photography experience. We provide everything needed, including cameras, lighting, and remote controls for capturing the perfect shot.

Contact Us

For more information or to book a session, you can reach out to us through our website or visit the studio in Albay. Follow us on social media to stay updated on promotions and events.
